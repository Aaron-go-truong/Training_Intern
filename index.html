Frequently Asked Questions
How do I qualify for a Sharia compliant home loan?
Despite following the Islamic Banking system, Afiyah is still bound by Australian lending legislation. All of our financial products are governed by the National Credit Consumer Protection Act - your financial health is protected by the Australian Government.
We take multiple factors into consideration when determining your ability to borrow money. Speak to one of our advisors today to learn more about how we evaluate each and every application.
What investments are Sharia compliant?
Islamic Banking does not allow investments into securities that generate money off interest related payments. Afiyah does not invest your money into traditional bonds but rather private equity funds and other related products that are Sharia compliant.
Does Afiyah hold a credit licence?
Yes. We follow all relevant legislation. We follow Islamic Banking principles to the best of our ability within the current legislative framework.
